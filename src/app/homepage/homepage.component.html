As the tagline of the company is “Its ME, For You” it clearly conveys the company’s message to its customers. We the ME team work for planning and management an event according to customer suitability as customer satisfaction is our prime importance. We believe in making personal relation with our customers in addition to professional one’s. Our team start working on all aspects of any event from the day when our customer just think of having it , so that our customer can be a guest at their own event and can enjoy every moment of the event without any worry . We are a team of young generation professional who have grown up in traditional family, so we know the importance of both rituals and try to create a blend of both rituals style. We the ME team never settles for creating a normal event, we always try to create a wow moment.
